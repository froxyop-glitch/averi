About

Where design discipline meets systems thinking

My work sits at the intersection of three usually-separate worlds: how something looks and feels, how it moves data safely, and how it's built underneath. Most projects only get one of those right — I aim for all three.

That background in networking and cybersecurity shapes how I approach even purely visual work: nothing ships without asking what happens when it's misused, attacked, or scaled past what it was designed for.
